Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cost or fee to apply?

Just your soul, but let's face it, that's a small price to pay.

What are the job benefits?

ICE offers competitive salaries, health/dental/vision, retirement plan, and generous paid leave. You'll also receive complimentary moral compartmentalization training, a subscription to our award-winning internal newsletter The Blind Eye, and access to our wellness program: Don't Think About It™.

What happens after I apply?

We will move fast. Once you submit your application you may be required to take an assessment. Buy it doesn't matter, we'll take anybody. Bonus if you're a failed cop or couldn't cut it in the military.

Will I be required to move?

Maybe, but ICE is flexible with duty locations. The people you encounter, however, will be moved whether they want to be or not.

What characteristics is ICE looking for?

Cowardice. Apathy. A willingness to dehumanize others in order to sleep at night. A strong desire to be part of a team, even if that team is doing morally reprehensible things. A high tolerance for cognitive dissonance. And a willingness to murder US citizen while filming them.

How dangerous is the work?

ICE officers should expect a certain level of risk when performing their duties. The physical risks are well-managed. The existential risks are between you and whatever you believe in.

Can recent graduates with no experience apply?

Yes! We love recent graduates. You're idealistic enough to believe you can work within the system to change it, and we respect that. Statistically, you will not.

Is an undergraduate degree required?

Please read each job listing for specific requirements. What we are looking for, more than credentials, is someone who has made peace with working outside the law and a willingness to violate the fourth amendment on a regular basis.
